Reading

Reading is a set of practices and procedures for working with a written text and the process of working with a text itself, aimed at extracting information from it, at perceiving the text and understanding it.

What you need:
  • 🐾 Read regularly.
  • 🐾 Read with a notepad at hand.
  • 🐾 Read Thoughtfully.
  • 🐾 Draw conclusions about what you read.
